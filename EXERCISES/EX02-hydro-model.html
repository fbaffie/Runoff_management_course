Hint: you can download data from there: https://www.nve.no/hydrologi/hydrologiske-data/ With this link, snow water equivalent is only available for the past 2 months at Anestoelen Check Senorge and Xgeo to get longer term data

Link between groundwater variation and discharge recession

Experiment with relationships between the drop in groundwater level at Anestoelen during the winter, and the recession in discharge at the outlet of Dalavatnet.
