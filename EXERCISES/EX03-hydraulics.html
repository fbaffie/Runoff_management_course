NVE decided to build a riprap protection on the stretch of stream bank affected. Discuss the potential location of this riprap and calculate its necessary height to protect against a 200 year flood. Hint: Nevina helps you define the catchment area and the 200-year flood. Hint: The riprap protection will have a very controlled geometry, making it easy to use open channel formulas.
