Take home messages

  • Norway is quite prone to unexpected flood flowpaths
  • Think worst case scenario: is everything “failsafe”
  • Look for local knowledge
  • Identify critical points in the infrastructure
  • Use NVE web tools to do a preliminary study of your catchment
  • This should usually be complemented by a walk-over to verify existing infrastructure, natural and built constraints
