Interesting to see a threshold for pollution control in Germany: “Maintenance of the already existing treatment methods is considered as a main priority in Germany. The treatment of runoff from roads is considered a requirement when the Annual Daily Traffic (ADT) exceeds 15,000 vehicles/day. Based on the recipient, treatment of runoff from roads is still required when the ADT is in the range of 2,000 -15,000. If the ADT value is less than 2,000 treatment of the runoff is not considered as mandatory.”
